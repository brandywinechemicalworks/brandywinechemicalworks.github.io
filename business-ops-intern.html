Brandywine Chemical Works

Business Operations Research Intern

This internship focuses on supplier research, toll‑manufacturing analysis, and market mapping for specialty intermediates. You’ll help evaluate production partners, analyze cost structures, and support commercial strategy.

You’ll work directly with the founder and gain exposure to real‑world chemical supply chain decisions, vendor qualification, and operational planning.

Responsibilities

  • Research suppliers and toll manufacturers
  • Analyze cost structures and production pathways
  • Support market and competitive mapping
  • Assist with documentation and vendor outreach

Preferred Skills

  • Strong analytical and research ability
  • Interest in chemicals, manufacturing, or supply chain
  • Clear written communication
