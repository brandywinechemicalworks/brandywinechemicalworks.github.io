Technical Writing & Documentation Intern

This internship focuses on creating documentation that supports operations, quality control, and customer communication. You’ll help build COA templates, SOP drafts, and technical summaries for specialty intermediates.

This role is ideal for someone who enjoys clear communication, structured writing, and translating technical concepts.

Responsibilities

  • Create COA and QC documentation templates
  • Draft SOPs and internal process documents
  • Write technical summaries for intermediates
  • Support customer‑facing documentation

Preferred Skills

  • Strong writing and clarity
  • Interest in chemistry or engineering
  • Attention to detail
