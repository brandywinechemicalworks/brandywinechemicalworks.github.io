Brandywine Chemical Works

Software Engineering Intern

As a Software Engineering Intern at Brandywine Chemical Works, you will help build internal tools, automation systems, and digital infrastructure that support our specialty chemical operations. You’ll work directly with the founder on real, production‑impacting systems.

This role is ideal for someone who enjoys building lightweight applications, improving workflows, and solving practical engineering problems with code.

Responsibilities

  • Develop internal tools and dashboards
  • Automate repetitive workflows and data handling
  • Support website improvements and backend logic
  • Work with real operational data and constraints

Preferred Skills

  • Python, JavaScript, or similar languages
  • Basic understanding of APIs or automation
  • Interest in engineering, manufacturing, or operations
