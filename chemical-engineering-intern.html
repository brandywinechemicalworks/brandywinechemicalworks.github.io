Chemical Engineering Intern

As a Chemical Engineering Intern, you will support process analysis, documentation, and evaluation of specialty intermediates. This role blends technical problem‑solving with real operational constraints.

You’ll gain exposure to process flow, equipment considerations, QC workflows, and supplier qualification.

Responsibilities

  • Assist with process and equipment analysis
  • Support documentation and COA/SDS review
  • Evaluate specialty intermediates and production pathways
  • Help build QC workflows and testing logic

Preferred Skills

  • Chemical engineering coursework
  • Interest in process design or manufacturing
  • Strong analytical and documentation skills
